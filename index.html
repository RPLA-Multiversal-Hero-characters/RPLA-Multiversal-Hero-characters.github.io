While RPLAs are increasingly deployed in applications ranging from in- teractive storytelling to task-oriented dialogue, there remains a significant gap in evaluating their knowledge, characteristic across multiple character versions.We introduce a benchmark to evaluate Role-Playing Language Agents (RPLAs) in multiversal scenarios inspired by 56 versions of 20 iconic Marvel and DC hero characters.
